Fried Chicken Wings

Ingredients

For the Wings:

  • 2 lbs (about 900g) chicken wings, split at the joint, tips removed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste for spice)
  • Cooking oil (for frying or baking)

For the Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (white or apple cider)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional garnish: chopped parsley or green onions

Instructions:

Preparing the Wings:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) if baking or heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C) if frying.
  2. Pat dry the chicken wings with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
  4. Season the chicken wings evenly with the spice mixture.

Baking Method:

  1. Place the seasoned chicken wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, flipping halfway through, until crispy and golden brown.

Frying Method:

  1. Carefully place the seasoned chicken wings in the hot oil, working in batches to avoid overcrowding the pot.
  2. Fry for about 10-12 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through. Use a thermometer to ensure the internal temperature of the wings reaches 165°F (75°C).
  3. Remove the wings from the oil and place them on a wire rack or paper towels to drain excess oil.

Making the Sauce (Optional):

  1. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the hot sauce, honey, vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Taste the sauce and adjust seasoning as needed with salt.
  5. Toss the cooked chicken wings in the sauce until they're evenly coated.

Serving:

  1. Arrange the wings on a serving platter and garnish with chopped parsley or green onions if desired.
  2. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce, celery sticks, and carrot sticks.

Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken wings!
